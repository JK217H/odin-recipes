Avocado Toast

Description

Well, you might be wondering why I make a recipe for something so simple like putting avocado on toast.

But as you will see there are a different ways to make avocado toast and I'll show you a few tips and tricks.

Heard enough?... Then let's get to it!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado (duh)
  • 2 Toast (whole grain or not is up to you)
  • Salt (I use herbal salt)
  • Black Pepper

Steps

How I make it:

  1. Put your toast in the toaster
  2. Meanwhile peel your avocado and cut it into slices
  3. Now put the pieces on the toast and add salt and pepper
  4. Voila you're done!

The other way (mashed):

  1. Put your toast in the toaster
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork
  3. Now put the avocado on the toast and add salt and pepper
  4. Voila you're done!
