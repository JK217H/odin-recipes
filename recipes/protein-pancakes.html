Protein Pancakes

Description

With this recipe you can make healthy high protein pancakes without giving up the taste of sugary "normal" pancakes.

These are really easy to make and shouldn't take too long. You can also put these in the fridge and eat later.

Are you convinced? ... Then let's go and make em!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 160 grams of milk
  • 30 grams of any Protein Powder (preferably vanilla)
  • oats 130 g (before adding these, blend them in a blender to get oat flour)
  • 5 grams of baking soda
  • add 3 table spoons of erythritol
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of cinnamon
  • extra virgin olive oil (can also use other frying oil)

Garnish with:

  • Maple syrup (no added sugar), blueberries, bananas, strawberries, etc.

Steps

  1. blend the oats in a blender to get oat meal
  2. mix the oat meal with the protein powder with a mixer
  3. slowly add the egg and the milk and keep mixing
  4. put the rest of the ingredients in and mix till you get a fluid dough
  5. check for taste (seasonings) and turn on a pan and add the extra virgin olive oil
  6. slowly make one pancake at a time in the pan on medium heat (use about 1 and a half table spoons of dough per pancake)
  7. Voila, there are you're delicious healthy high protein pancakes!
