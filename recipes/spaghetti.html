Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Is there anything better than a plate of Spaghetti Bolognese?

I don't think so, as this is my favorite dish of all time, ever since my mother first made it.

I'm now gonna show you how to make the best Spaghetti Bolognese... you ready???

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of ground beef
  • 500 grams of spaghetti
  • 1 table sponn of frying oil
  • 400 grams of tomato sauce
  • 30 grams of tomato paste
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with hot water, salt it and bring it to boil
  2. Then add the spaghetti into it and let these sit for about 10 minutes
  3. Meanwhile put 1 table spoon of frying oil in a wide pot and add the beef after it is hot
  4. Let the beef brown for 2 minutes and salt it
  5. Now add the tomato paste and 200 millilitres of water
  6. Mix it well and then add the tomato paste and salt it
  7. Bring it to boil and then turn the heat to low and lit it sit for about 15 min
  8. When the spaghetti are done rinse the water and add a little bit of butter
  9. Now put these both on a plate and voila you're done!!!
